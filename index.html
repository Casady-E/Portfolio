Portfolio

En Garde is a concept company/mobile application that I created as a personal project. The brand identity behind the app was extremely important in driving the design direction of the project. I wanted to create an identity that was modern, simplistic, and memorable. To achieve this, I went through a lengthy concepting process that included researching other popular fencing brands, sketching different logos, and working with font pairings. Since the main feature of this project was the mobile application, I needed to create a brand that would easily transition into a mobile setting. A core feature of the app is the ability to challenge anyone else who has the app to a fencing duel. For that reason, I included the clashing swords as the main feature of the logo.

This project was created during my time at Studio M, a service learning internship through Metropolitan State University Of Denver. Cheltenham Elementary School (CES) is located in the Denver Public School (DPS) system and serves an extremely diverse student body. CES reached out to Studio M for a complete rebranding and beautification of the school. After meeting with the client multiple times, we decided on pursuing a superhero and comic book aesthetic for the school to create an engaging and fun environment for the students. I was tasked with creating multiple banners that would be hung around the fence line of the school to draw in new families. I also developed an environmental system that emphasizes the importance of school through ten different creative comic style bubbles. The message behind these designs is to empower the students at the school and drive their desire to learn.
