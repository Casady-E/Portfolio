En Garde App & Brand Design

Client

Personal Project

Role

Product Designer

Software

Photoshop // Illustrator // Adobe XD

Link to Interactive Prototype

En Garde is a concept company/mobile application that I created as a personal project. The core feature of the En Garde app is to locate nearby fencers and challenge them to a duel. The map allows you to locate the nearest piste to conduct the duel and allows you to see anyone who is actively searching for a match. The chat screen is also a core feature of the app to increase community engagement. The chat screen is accessible on every screen throughout the app so that the user can constantly be in contact with their friends or group. The app also features a ranking system, a replay page, a news page, and an events page.
