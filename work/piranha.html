Piranha

Client

Piranha Eyewear

Role

Graphic Designer

Software

Photoshop // Illustrator // After Effects

While working at Piranha Eyewear, I created several advertisements to announce the release of a new product and emphasize its unique features. My own photography is used for the Vance advertisement below. I developed a sticker series that was used as a free gift placed in every online order package before it was shipped out. The goal was to create something memorable and engaging that still fit brand guidelines. The three videos were created as quick advertisements for social media. These were meant to be short but concise while still enticing and informing the customer of new product lines. The display dividers were for a stand up sunglasses display. Since there were so many different styles under the Piranha brand, I created many unique designs while also developing most of the branding for each style.
