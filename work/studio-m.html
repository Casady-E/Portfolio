Studio M

Client

Cheltenham Elementary // T.A.C.T.

Role

Visual Designer // Project Manager

Software

Photoshop // Illustrator

These projects were created during my time at Studio M, a service learning internship through Metropolitan State University Of Denver. Cheltenham Elementary School (CES) is located in  the Denver Public School (DPS) system and serves a diverse student body. CES reached out to Studio M for a complete rebranding and beautification of the school. After meeting with the client multiple times, we decided on pursuing a superhero and comic book aesthetic for the school to create an engaging and fun environment for the students. I was tasked with creating multiple banners that would be hung around the fence line of the school to draw in new families. I also developed an environmental system that emphasizes the importance of school through ten different creative comic style bubbles. T.A.C.T. is a local nonprofit that teaches trades to people on the autism spectrum. They also came to Studio M for a complete rebranding and wanted us to develop a system of signs to help direct students around the building. Another team member developed the logo and I created this set of signs to coincide with the aesthetic of the brandmark.
